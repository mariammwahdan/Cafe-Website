About Us

Coffee Addicts is a shop of coffee people passionate about specialty coffee and all the fun gear and equipment that go with it. We started in 2020, mostly selling espressso and turkish coffee to an emerging crowd of specialty coffee baristas and home coffee enthusiasts. As the market grew, we expanded our line of products considerably.

In our pursuit of providing an all-encompassing coffee experience, we've cultivated a vibrant community around Coffee Addict. Beyond being a haven for quality coffee, we've hosted events, workshops, and tasting sessions to foster a deeper connection with our customers. As we continue to grow, our commitment to sustainability has become a cornerstone of our ethos. From ethically sourced beans to eco-friendly packaging, we strive to make every cup not just delightful for your taste buds but also for the planet.
